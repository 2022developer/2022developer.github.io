How to Teach Kids?

All Lessons are only for 5-10 Minutes, and with Flashcard Technique. It has covered more than 25-50 words in just short time. So even your kids watch for 10 minutes they will learn lots of words.

Show each lessons 2 - 3 times in one or two days and kids will naturally learn to read like they learn mother tongue.

1 or 2 Years Old Child - Make them watch just Level 1 all the Lessons one time daily and repeat for a week. They will learn Reading naturally by the time they will turn 2 or 3 years old

3 or 4 years old child - Get then watch each lessons two times daily

5 to 7 years old Kids - Get them watch each Lessons 2 time daily and pause the lesson and write them in a book for better practice and understanding. This will help them to develop a habit of self learning.
